# Kellanova (kellanova)

Kellanova is a global snacks-led consumer packaged foods company, formed in 2023 from the spin-off of the Kellogg Company's snacks, international cereal and noodles, and North American frozen breakfast businesses. Kellanova owns and markets brands including Pringles, Cheez-It, Pop-Tarts, Eggo, RXBAR, MorningStar Farms, and Special K outside North America.
